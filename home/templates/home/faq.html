FAQs

We hope we can answer some of your questions here.
If not, please get in touch with us.

+44 0689 356874

loveplants@example.com


  • Do you ship to my country?

    For orders from Love Plants, we ship to people in the UK: England, Northern Ireland, Republic of Ireland, Scotland and Wales.

  • Can you ship to a PO Box box?

    No, we don’t ship to PO Box addresses.

  • Can I change my shipping address?

    No because we send our products out quickly, so your order may be on its way to you.

  • If I place an order today, when will I get it?

    We’ll ship your order as soon as it’s available. We’ll send you a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number so you’ll know when it’s on its way. Delivery usually takes 7-10 days.

  • Can you gift wrap my order?

    No, we don’t offer gift-wrapping. Plant are packaged very specifically and carefully to avoid damage during transit.

  • How do I cancel my order?

    Please contact us as +44 0689 356874 via telephone or loveplants@example.com via email to cancel your order.

  • What is your return and refund policy?

    We issue refunds on a case-by-case basis. If you’d like to return your order for a refund, please email our Customer Service team at loveplants@example.com within 30 days of getting your order, and include your 32-digit order reference number.

  • What can I do if my order arrives damaged?

    We’re sorry your order arrived damaged. Please email our Customer Service team within 30 days of getting your order, and include your order number.

  • What can I do if I don’t get an order confirmation email?

    Please check your email spam or junk folder in case your order confirmation is in there. If not, please email our Customer Service team and include your order number.
