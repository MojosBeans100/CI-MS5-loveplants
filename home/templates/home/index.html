Houseplants add a natural beauty to your home, and can bring any room to life. You can choose larger floor plants for instant impact, or scatter smaller potted plants amongst your personal items for bursts of natural colour


We stock houseplants from around the world, from simple ferns which love cooler climates, to more exotic numbers such as the Pothos plant, which originate from warm, misty rainforests

Easy plant babies

Look for easy to propagate plants to multiply your collection fast.


These plants produce lots of 'babies' or 'pups' which can be separated from the mother plant. Let the roots sit in water until the pup has established a storng enough root system to support itself.


Soon you'll have another strong, healthy plant which can be planted into it's own pot.


These plants really are the gift that keeps on giving.
