Easy plant babies

We stock lots of easy to propagate plants to multiply your collection fast.


These plants produce lots of 'babies' or 'pups' which can be separated from the mother plant. Let the roots sit in water until the pup has established a strong enough root system to support itself.


Soon you'll have another strong, healthy plant which can be planted into it's own pot.


These plants really are the gift that keeps on giving.

Going on holiday?

Nobody wants to come home from a well-earned break to discover that half their beautiful plant collection has perished.


Look for low maintenance houseplants which even the most scatter-brained plant-lover cannot kill!


There are many plants which can survive, and even thrive, with significant neglect from their owner. In particular, succulents are a good choice because they do not cope with being regularly watered.
