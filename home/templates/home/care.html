House plant care guide

Grow thriving, healthy houseplants with the help of our care advice.

Houseplants in general are relatively simply to look after. They don't require as much attention as you might think!

Feel free to follow some of our handy tips for keeping them looking and feeling healthy.

Feed them

If you don’t feed your house plants, they won’t thrive. Start feeding regularly, around three months after you’ve planted them, as the nutrients present in the compost start to deplete after this time. Use a liquid feed diluted in water, following the instructions on the bottle. Most houseplants don’t need feeding more than once every one to three months, between March and September
